About

Located on a vibrant pedestrianised market street, BartleBee is a bright and airy space with glazed frontage on two sides and an outside terrace on the market. Serving our signature drinks menus, along with our freshly roasted coffee from the BartleBee roastery, our style is open, relaxed and friendly. Come down for a coffee with the morning paper, a cocktail and small plates in the evening and anything in between!
